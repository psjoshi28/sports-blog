India win SAFF Championship for 8th time; Sunil Chhetri equals Messi with 800 goals

India crushed Nepal 3-0 to win their eighth SAFF Championship title after second-half goals from Sunil Chhetri, Suresh Singh and Sahal Abdul Samad in the final

India crushed Nepal 3-0 to win their eighth SAFF Championship title after second-half goals from Sunil Chhetri, Suresh Singh and Sahal Abdul Samad in the final on Saturday. Chhetri scored in the 49th minute of the match, which put him level with Lionel Messi on 80 international goals, while Suresh Singh doubled the lead in the 50th to pave the way for India's title triumph, a much-needed after a series of mediocre outings. Sahal Abdul Samad made it 3-0 when he dribbled past a few defenders on the 90th minute.

India dominated possession in the first-half but failed to break the deadlock. However, Chhetri put India ahead minutes into the second half and even before Nepal could down settle down, Suresh made it 2-0. This is the Indian football team's first title triumph under head coach Igor Stimac, who faced criticism in recent times for his side's inability to win enough games.

India could have gone ahead minutes before the half-time but the ball that was delivered from the right flank for Chhetri was not met with properly as the Indian captain's his effort went wide. However, India more than made up for that with their second-half display, much to Stimac's delight.

Sportspersons from Indian Air Force who have made India proud

As the country celebrates the Indian Air Force (IAF) Day, here we look at some of the athletes from IAF who have made India proud at the global level.

It is a well-known fact that apart from their duties to the nation in times of crisis, the Indian defence forces are also a breeding ground for many talented sportspersons who go on to represent India at the highest of all stages. From the likes of Milkha Singh to the modern-day legend Neeraj Chopra, the defence forces have gifted India, countless sporting heroes. As the country celebrates the Indian Air Force (IAF) Day today i.e. 8th October 2021, here we look at some of the athletes from IAF who have made India proud at the global level.

Shikha Pandey
A cricketer par excellence, Shikha Pandey is an air traffic control officer with the Indian Air Force. A pace-bowling all-rounder by specialisation, the 30-year-old Pandey has represented India more than 100 times taking 118 wickets and scoring 773 runs.

Santosh kumar
Probably one of the least known names in this list, Santosh Kumar is a Wushu practitioner. A sergeant in the Indian Air Force, Santosh clinched the bronze medal in men's 56kg sanda at the 2018 Asian Games besides also having a World Championships gold in his kitty.
